Michael is the Regional Manager at Dunder Mifflin, a fun loving and charismatic man who likes to put work aside in order to have fun. While his love for entertainment often results in trouble and danger, he's always set on making life convienent

Michael lives for others and their views of him, treating his office staff like family and doing whatever he can to earn their love and appreciation. A bit of a narcissistic man, but one who lives for love nonetheless

Dwight Lightbox

Dwight Schrute: "Today, smoking is going to save lives"
X

Dwight is Michael's assistant to the Regional Manager, a made up position because Dwight has no real leadership in the office. Dwight is a loyal worker and the top salesman at the company, but he can also be a bit of a suck up to Michael

Dwight is a strong rule follower who wants a well working office with no distractions, making him and Jim enemies. While Dwight tends to take things to the extreme, he's still a strong worker who will do whatever it takes to make it to the top

Jim Lightbox

Jim Halpert: *Looks at camera*
X

Jim is the person in the office who just doesn't care. He does his job as does it well, but he spends a large portion of his time just relaxing, having fun, and pulling pranks, especially on Dwight. While Jim cares about his job, he also doesn't take it too seriously

Jim also spends his time with Pam in a culture defining love story, as they grow together and work and mess around together, working together once again often to prank Dwight. While Jim does well at his job, he always aspires to more, and being second in command in the office isn't always quite enough

Pam Lightbox

Pam Beesly: "There's a lot of beauty in ordinary things"
X

Pam is a secretary turned saleswoman who does what she can to feel satisfied in her career. From becoming a saleswoman to going to art school, Pam tries to work her way up the ranks while doing what she needs to do to work and make a living

Pam is a caring a fun loving person, often participating in office fun and activities alongside Jim, including pranking Dwight. Pam is often the voice of reason and the one who sets the limits on things while still opting for a good time

Andy Lightbox

Andy Bernard: "I went to Cornell"
X

Andy is a graduate from Cornell, and he'll let you know it. Andy is an alright salesmen who came from another branch of Dunder Mifflin and immediately started working his way to the top. While Andy has aspirations, he isn't the strongest worker

Andy is usually a pretty fun and relaxed person, but he can also flip very quickly. Andy is easily fooled and can have some pretty bad reactions to things, which is why he went through anger management. Though he can be a bit of a ticking time bomb, Andy is usually a fun loving and relaxed guy

Angela Lightbox

Angela Martin: "Save Bandit!"

Angela is a talented accountant who does her job well, but she can be kind of uptight. She's a very big rule follower and doesn't like a messy or chaotic office, she prefers to just get the work done. While Angela may be a bit of an opposition to much of the office, it can't be denied that she has talent in her work

Angela may try to appear the way that she does, but in reality she can be a very caring person. Angela is a mother of many cats and a good friend and colleague to many in the office. Angela is tough and gets the work done when it's needed, but she is also very passionate about those she cares about
